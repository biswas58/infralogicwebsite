How We Work

Rigorous process.
Flexible delivery.

01 —

Discovery & Audit

We begin with a thorough environment assessment — architecture diagrams, traffic analysis, dependency mapping, and stakeholder interviews. No assumptions, only data.

02 —

Design & Proposal

Our engineers produce a detailed design document with 2–3 architecture options, cost modeling, risk trade-offs, and a phased delivery plan for your review.

03 —

Implementation

We execute in controlled, reversible phases — staging first, production last — with continuous status reporting and integration testing at every gate.

04 —

Handoff & Enablement

Full run-book delivery, ops team training, 30-day hypercare support, and optional retainer for ongoing advisory. You're never left holding undocumented infrastructure.
